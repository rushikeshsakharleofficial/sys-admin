# Responsive Checklist

## Viewports

Test at least:

- 1440x900 desktop
- 1366x768 laptop
- 1024x768 tablet
- 390x844 mobile large
- 360x640 mobile small

## Checks

- No horizontal scroll unless intentional.
- Header controls remain reachable.
- Sidebar becomes drawer or remains usable.
- Footer stacks correctly.
- Cards reflow correctly.
- Tables scroll or convert to cards.
- Floating buttons do not cover submit/CTA.
- Modals fit screen and scroll internally.
- Dropdowns stay inside viewport.
- Form fields remain readable.
- Text does not clip.
- Images preserve aspect ratio.
- Touch targets are large enough.
- Sticky elements do not consume too much height.

## Mobile special checks

- Hamburger opens and closes.
- Drawer focus is managed.
- Body scroll lock works when drawer/modal is open.
- Soft keyboard does not hide focused input where testable.
- Bottom fixed UI does not block form submit.
